Pick 2 primes, P and Q.

The values of P and Q generate N and Z.

Find the number N = P*Q (clock size)

Find the number Z = (P-1)*(Q-1)

Then you need to find two numbers E and D such that E*D mod Z = 1.

Enter a candidate value of K into the box.

Factors of K:


Use factors of candidate K values for E and D. E and D should be relatively prime.
